Saegis Library Rules and Regulations

Membership Procedure and Opening Hours

  1. All Saegis Campus students who are duly registered for Programme of Study not less than six months duration shall have access to the library free of charge.
  2. Library Membership Forms can be obtained from the library counter.
  3. Library will be kept open every day from 8.00 am to 5.00 pm. It will be closed on Poya days and Mercantile Holidays.

Library Usage / Lending Period and Returning Procedures

  1. Books in the Lending Section can be taken for two weeks and if the book is not reserved can be extended for further period. Library materials overdue charges will be imposed on late returns. (Rs 20/- per day)
  2. Books in the Permanent Reference Section are not available for lending.
  3. Books in the Scheduled Reference Section are issued only for 24 hours, if not returned on the following day, a fine will be imposed. (Rs 50) per day.
  4. Permanent Reference books and periodicals will be issued only for photocopying. Max periods of 2 Hours
  5. Renewal of borrowings and reservation facilities are available.
  6. Books should not be disfigured or scribbled by readers. If any marks are already in the book, readers should point them out to the librarian. In the absence of such a report, the book issued will be considered a perfect copy.
  7. Borrowers are responsible for the prompt return in good condition of all materials borrowed. The penalty for a damaged book is the full cost of its replacement plus a fine.
  8. Every student is required to return all borrowed library books at the completion of studentship.

General Library Rules

  1. Students should produce their ID card whenever they enter the library or asked or while in the library.
  2. Students are not permitted to bring food and drinks or any other personal belonging in to the library.
  3. Valuable items such as cash or jewellery should not be kept in the baggage counters. Library staff will not be responsible for loss of such materials.
  4. Readers are not allowed to hold discussions in the library.
  5. Library seats are not allowed to be reserved.
  6. Students should turn off their mobile phones while they are inside the library.
  7. All students must adhere to the Rules & Regulations of Saegis Campus in general and the library rules and regulations in particular.
  8. All students should be properly attired when they enter the library.
